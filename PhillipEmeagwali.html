Dr. Phillip Emeagwali

Life: 1954 - Present

Significance

Emeagwali is known for his work on what was at the time the fastest computer in the world, achieved by linking thousands of computers together in parallel to perform nearly 3.1 billion calculations per second.

History

Dr. Phillip Emeagwali is a computer scientist hailing from Nigeria, with expertise and experience in various fields such as mathematics, computer science, and engineering. Additionally, he describes himself as a 'public intellectual', and has a sense of duty to educate the African community and advocate for their civil rights. He is still a prominent figure in computer science today, and will be remembered as one of the brightest minds in the field.
